            • Project 2: SaverPal

          • This is a personal financial management app aimed at enhancing the lives of sole propriators by ensuring a good balance between business finances and personal finances. This is driven by the fact that most sole propriatorship businesses barely make it to the end of the 3rd year of business due to mixing of personal and business finances.

            • Project 3: PesaMastah

          • This is a Financial accounting app that is paired with a financial accounting education feature aimed at SME businesses. This is driven by the fact that over 60% of all SME's in Africa do not keep good financial records due to :
            • Unavailability of affordable financial accounting tools.
            • Available toold being too complex for a business owner without a financial accounting background to use.
            • Lack of knowledge in financial accounting practices.
